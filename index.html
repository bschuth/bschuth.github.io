Rabbit Hole with Dance

Last week I suddenly watched a lot of dance, via a roundabout inspiration. My brother asked me for some suggestions about pieces by Philip Glass that might interest his who is on-spectrum. That got me thinking about Glass again, which got me thinking about Steve Reich, and thinking about Reich reminded me of the piece that the Dutch choreographer Anna Teresa de Keersmaeker created using Reich’s Violin Phase.. That also got me thinking about Lucinda Child’s choreography for Glass' Einstein on the Beach, which totally hypnotized me when I saw that work at BAM back in the 1990s.

First Post

I expect to place short notes here, something of a public journal. I’m writing from Eastport, where the weather took a turn from the feverish to the sharply crisp on Thursday night. As I was struggling to sleep, the north wind suddenly rose up, sounding at first like a locomotive, or the shock wave from an explosion. When it became clear there was no fireball behind the breeze, but instead only colder air, I was able to drift off after wrapping myself more closely in my comforter.
